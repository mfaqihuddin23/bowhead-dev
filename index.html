Your Data Belongs to You

Easy-to-use data ownership means you get to decide in which projects to participate in, built on the principle that it is your data, and you control the rules using smart contracts.

HealthDAO is a decentralized organization that connects life science research to data and funding. Every patient is different and we believe every single patient data point has value. By using the blockchain we are able to tokenize participation in a collaborative, privacy-by-design way to power the breakthroughs of tomorrow.
