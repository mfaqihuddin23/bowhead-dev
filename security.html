Your health data rooted in privacy

We are a healthcare technology company focusing on innovations that guarantee individuals the self-sovereign ownership of their health data.

On-Chain encryption key management

Apple, Google and most apps have full unencrypted access to view your data. Bowhead’s ecosystem gives users their encryption key thus grantig self-sovereign ownership. Only these keys can decrypt the data. This is all verifiable on-chain and open - source.

Health meets Cryptography

Bowhead,s ecosystem leverages Ethereum’s Smart Contracts for data interaction and Interplanetary File System (IPFS) for decentralized storage.
