Our Story

Bowhead,s founders, Dr.Francisco Diaz-Mitoma, Dr. Rhea Mehta and Francisco Diaz-Mitoma Jr.were building a smart dispenser for supplements and then one of thrm went to their doctor and asked for their health records. The doctor said those records belonged to their clinic and they couldn’t share them easily. That summer they started building a blockchain team and launched a pilot with 700 patients in switzerland, Germany and Austria. Empowering them to own and control their records. Since then we have helped over 130,000 people create their digital health vaults.
